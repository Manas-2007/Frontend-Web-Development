How To Use

This section helps new voters understand how to use the dashboard and complete voting correctly.

VOTER GUIDE

Step 1 — Check Your Dashboard

Review your voter profile and current voting status before proceeding.

01

CHECK

Name and Voter ID

CHECK

Whether you already voted

Step 2 — Confirm Voting is Live

Voting can be done only when the admin has started the election session.

02

LOOK FOR

The LIVE status indicator in the top navbar

If voting is not live, the voter should wait until the election is started by the admin.

Step 3 — Open Election Parties

Move to the Election Parties tab to see all available parties and their symbols.

03

YOU WILL SEE

Party cards with symbol, party name, and vote button

Step 4 — Vote Carefully

Select your party carefully before confirming your final vote.

04

IMPORTANT

Once you click the vote button, a confirmation popup will appear before final submission.

Step 5 — Vote is Locked After Submission

The system follows a one-person, one-vote rule.

05

After a successful vote, your dashboard will update the vote status, vote date, vote time, and vote count. You will not be able to cast a second vote.
