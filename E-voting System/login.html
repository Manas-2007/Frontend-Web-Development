About Online Voting System

The Online Voting System provides a secure and transparent platform where elections are conducted digitally. Only administrators can initiate voting sessions, ensuring complete control and fairness.

Once voting begins, registered voters can log in and cast their vote safely. Individual votes remain hidden, while only final results are visible after voting ends.

This system improves efficiency, eliminates manual errors, and guarantees confidentiality and integrity during elections.
